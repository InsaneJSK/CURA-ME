FAQ for Cura Me
1. What is Cura Me?
Cura Me is a health application designed to provide users with personalized medicine recommendations based on reported symptoms. It also suggests nearby hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies, along with potential causes and treatments.

2. How does Cura Me work?
Users input their symptoms and any known allergies. The app analyzes this information and provides tailored medicine recommendations, alternative options for those with allergies, and nearby healthcare facilities.

3. Is Cura Me a substitute for professional medical advice?
No, Cura Me is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. It is designed to assist users in finding relevant information and should always be used in conjunction with advice from healthcare professionals.

4. How accurate are the medicine recommendations?
The recommendations are based on a combination of medical databases and algorithms. While we strive for high accuracy, individual responses to medication can vary, so it's important to consult a doctor or pharmacist before making any changes to your treatment.

5. What should I do if I experience side effects from a recommended medicine?
If you experience any side effects, stop taking the medicine and seek immediate medical attention. Report any adverse reactions to your healthcare provider.

6. How does Cura Me ensure the reliability of its information?
Cura Me utilizes reputable medical sources and guidelines to generate recommendations. Our team continuously updates the database to reflect current medical knowledge and practices.

7. Can I trust the nearby hospitals and clinics suggested by Cura Me?
The app provides a list of nearby healthcare facilities based on user location. We source this information from public databases, but we recommend checking the facility's reviews and credentials before visiting.

10. How can I provide feedback or report issues with the app?

11. Are there any costs associated with using Cura Me?

12. What should I do in a medical emergency?
In case of a medical emergency, please call emergency services or visit the nearest hospital immediately. Cura Me is not designed for emergency situations.
